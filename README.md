# Sales Analysis Project
## Overview

### This project analyzes sales data to identify trends, top-performing products, customer behavior, and revenue patterns.

###  Objectives
### . Analyze monthly sales performance
### . Identify top-selling products
### . Evaluate regional sales trends
### . Generate actionable business insights
###   Tools Used
### . Python
### . Pandas
### . NumPy
### . Matplotlib / Seaborn
### . Jupyter Notebook
###  Key Insights
### . Total revenue increased by X% over the period.
### . Product A generated the highest sales.
### . Region B contributed the largest share of revenue.
### . Sales peaked during specific months.
